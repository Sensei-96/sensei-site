Capabilities

Autonomy, supervised

Every capability ships with its own restraint. Agents act — verifiers check, policies deny by default, humans hold the final gate.

Goal Engine

Watches signals — legal-source changes, market events, system anomalies — and turns them into goals. Each goal is risk-classified: LOW and MEDIUM run automatically and are logged; HIGH waits for explicit human approval in Telegram.

signal → goal → risk → execute

Verifier Swarm

Constitutional, anti-corruption and regulatory verifiers run on every output in parallel. An answer that fails verification never leaves the system — it is repaired or escalated.

safety gate · L2

Self-improvement, human-gated

Workflows evolve by genetic programming, prompts improve through A/B tests, new skills are proposed from execution traces. No self-modification is ever applied without a human approval step.

propose, never apply

Legal & market skills

Production clients for Armenian legal sources — ARLIS, DataLex, Parliament, eGov — plus vector retrieval, document processing, and execution adapters for trading venues.

Security & compliance

Rules that outrank the model

The constitution is code-adjacent and inviolable: the system may propose changes to its own rules, but only a human can activate them.

Constitutional core

Hard constraints with top-down precedence: human override wins, HIGH risk needs approval, no self-modification of L0 prompts, accuracy over bravado.

Deny by default

Tool allowlists, egress allowlists, token and cost caps, wall-time limits, PII redaction — enforced by the policy engine before any task reaches a sandbox.

Sovereign data

GDPR and Armenian DPA compliance; Armenian personal data and case content stay inside the sovereign perimeter. Every decision lands in a tamper-evident audit chain.
